THE NEW ALBUM. HELLFIRE

RELEASE DATE: 31TH MAY 2024

Hellfire is the debut studio album by Brazilian heavy metal/hard rock band Shock Hazard.
Announced in the Mariutti Team Zine. The album marks the band's first release in 1 year
of existence. Shock Hazard is Beto Conde, Dipaula Machado, and Christopher Wyll.
